About

The Math Open At Andover is an annual mathematics competition for middle schoolers, hosted by Phillips Academy Andover in Andover, Massachusetts. Although MOAA is traditionally held on campus, MOAA 2020 will be held virtually due to COVID-19 concerns. Through MOAA, middle school students can experience mathematical exploration, collaboration, and competition. We hope to promote the involvement of middle school students in the mathematical community with a fulfilling, intellectually stimulating, and fun experience for everybody.

Schedule

What to expect on contest day

  • 10:30 AM

    Opening Ceremony

  • 11:15 AM

    General Round

    A team competition, the General Round is a 20 question test to be completed in 40 minutes. All answers are non-negative integers and no answer will have a value greater than 1,000,000.

  • 12:15 PM

    Theme Round

    A team competition, the Theme Round is a 20 question test to be completed in 40 minutes. All answers are non-negative integers and no answer will have a value greater than 1,000,000.

  • 1 PM

    Lunch Break

  • 2 PM

    Speakers

  • 4 PM

    Gunga Bowl

    Inspired by the Harvard MIT Mathematics Tournament's Guts Round, the Gunga Bowl is a fast-paced race between teams - with a live scoreboard and timer. The Gunga Bowl is composed of 9 rounds of 3 problems each, to be completed in 60 minutes. All answers are non-negative integers.

  • 5:15 PM

    Problem Discussion with Prof. Po-Shen Loh

  • 6:30 PM
    Closing
    Ceremony
